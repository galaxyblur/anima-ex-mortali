# Anima Ex Mortali

*Soul from mortality* - a space for creative work that acknowledges impermanence while pursuing meaning.

## Mission

To create, curate, and share threads of inquiry across disciplines.

## Values

- **Authenticity over polish** - Raw work has value
- **Process over product** - The journey matters
- **Connection over isolation** - Ideas exist in relation
