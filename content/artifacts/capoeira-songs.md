A data visualization tool that analyzes the lyrics of capoeira songs, surfacing word frequencies and n-grams (consecutive word combinations) as interactive visualizations.

## Features

- Word cloud weighted by frequency
- Top 10 most common words
- Top 10 most common n-grams
- Filters out words appearing fewer than 20 times
